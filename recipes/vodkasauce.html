Vodka Sauce Pasta

Description

Vodka sauce is a sauce made primarily with tomatoes and cream, where a small amount of vodka is added to bring out the flavors of the sauce. The alcohol in the sauce gets cooked off in the process, so this is safe to consume for those under the legal age limit for consumption of alcohol.

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Pasta (of any kind)
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Vodka
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Basil
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat up olive oil on a pan on medium heat.
  2. Dice up onions and garlic and cook until browned.
  3. Add canned tomatoes and tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add salt, black pepper, red pepper, and vodka and let reduce until thickened, about 20 minutes.
  5. Cook pasta according to directions while sauce is thickening.
  6. Blend the sauce, using either an immersion blender or normal one. Basil can be added before or after this step.
  7. Pour sauce back into pan if it was poured into blender. Turn heat on low, add heavy cream and cheese and stir until mixed.
  8. Serve over pasta
